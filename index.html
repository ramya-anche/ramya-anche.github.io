About Me

I am a postdoctoral research associate at the Steward Observatory, University of Arizona, working on polarimetry and high-contrast imaging of exoplanets. Previously, I was a WALOP instrument postdoc at IUCCA, India. I graduated with my Ph.D. from Indian Institute of Astrophysics (IIA) and University of Calcutta in Jan 2020 and in July 2014 with a Masters in Astronomical Instrumentation from IIA and University of Calcutta.
