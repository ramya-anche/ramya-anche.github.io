About Me

I am a Postdoctoral Researcher at the University of Arizona Space Astrophysics Lab (UASAL), Department of Astronomy and Steward Observatory, at the University of Arizona working on polarimetry and high-contrast imaging for current and future ground and space-based telescopes. Previously, I was a WALOP instrument postdoc at IUCCA, India.

I graduated with my Ph.D. in Applied Optics in January 2020 and Masters (MTech) in Astronomical Instrumentation in July 2014 from the Indian Institute of Astrophysics (IIA) and University of Calcutta, and Bachelors of Engineering (BE) in Electrical and Electronics from VTU, Belgaum.

In addition to research, I am passionate about science communication and conducting astronomy outreach events for students and the public.
