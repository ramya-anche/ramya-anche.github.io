About Me

I am a Postdoctoral Researcher at the University of Arizona Space Astrophysics Lab (UASAL), Department of Astronomy and Steward Observatory, at the University of Arizona working on polarimetry and high-contrast imaging for current/future ground and space-based telescopes. Previously, I was a WALOP instrument postdoc at IUCCA, India.

I graduated with my Ph.D. in Applied Optics in Jan 2020 and Masters (MTech) in Astronomical Instrumentation in July 2014 from Indian Institute of Astrophysics (IIA) and University of Calcutta, and Bachelors of Engineering (BE) in Electrical and Electronics from VTU, Belgaum.

In addition to research, I am interested in science communication and conducting Astronomy outreach events for students and the public.
